The Initiative for Venom Associated Microbes and Parasites (iVAMP) is a collaborative, open-source group of researchers worldwide of all career stages and types with the shared interest to expand the reach of each otherâ€™s work as well as the direction the host-microbe interaction field.

What are our long-term aims?

1) To establish a network of scientists interested in microbe- and parasite- venom interactions.
2) To contribute a database from collaborative research, free to the public.
3) To engage the general public with ongoing research through inquiry and discussion.
